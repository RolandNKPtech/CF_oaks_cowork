<p>A tummy tuck, also known as an abdominoplasty, is a surgical procedure that can help to flatten and tighten the abdominal area. This type of surgery can be helpful for people who have excess skin or fat in the abdominal area, or who have stretched or separated abdominal muscles due to pregnancy or weight changes.</p>
<p>The cost of a tummy tuck can vary depending on a number of factors, including the location of the surgery, the experience of the surgeon, and the specific needs of the patient. In general, the cost of a tummy tuck ranges from about $9,000 to $11,000. This price may be higher or lower depending on the specifics of the procedure.</p>
<p>There are a few different types of tummy tucks, and the cost can vary based on the type of procedure that is performed. For example, a mini tummy tuck is a more limited procedure that only focuses on the lower abdominal area, while a full tummy tuck involves more extensive surgery that addresses the entire abdominal area. A mini tummy tuck may cost less than a full tummy tuck, but it may not provide as much improvement in the appearance of the abdominal area.</p>
<p>At The Oaks Plastic Surgery our fees include hospital, anesthesia, garment and followups.</p>
<p>Overall, the cost of a tummy tuck can vary depending on a number of factors. It is important to discuss the cost of the procedure with a qualified surgeon and to carefully consider the potential benefits and risks before deciding to undergo a tummy tuck.</p>
